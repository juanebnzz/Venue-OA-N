FAQ

Questions we hear often.

Do you charge corkage?+

Never. Bring your own wine, champagne, spirits, or soft drinks. No corkage fees, no hidden charges. Stock up and celebrate exactly as you'd like.

Can I bring my own caterer?+

Absolutely. Venue Oaón is fully self-catering. Bring your own food, order from your favourite restaurant, or hire a caterer. Kitchen access is available on selected packages.

How do I get pricing?+

We don't publish pricing because every event is different — guest count, suite, package, and add-ons all affect the final quote. Fill in the form above and we'll come back to you with a full breakdown.

Can I book more than one suite?+

Yes — you can book multiple suites for the same time slot if your event needs more space. We'll discuss what makes sense when you enquire.

What time slots are available?+

We run two time slots per day — morning (08:00–13:00) and afternoon (14:00–19:00). Each slot gives you a freshly prepared space. We don't run back-to-back events in the same suite.

Is there parking?+

Yes — on-site secured parking for all guests. No street parking stress on the day of your event.

Do I need to use Oaón Events for décor?+

No. You're welcome to bring your own décor team. However, all our packages are offered through Oaón Events and we recommend them highly — they know the space well and the coordination is seamless.
